SafeGuardian AI Project

AI for Occupational Safety

Assisting in the development of SafeGuardian, an artificial intelligence system trained on OSHA regulatory standards to revolutionize hazard identification and make safety knowledge more accessible.

  • Conducting alpha and beta testing to evaluate how accurately the model interprets safety questions.
  • Identifying gaps in OSHA-related knowledge responses and testing real-world safety scenarios.
  • Bridging the gap between AI, regulatory knowledge, and practical safety application for workers and professionals.
